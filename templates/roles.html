How Role Weights Work
What are vote weights?

Vote weights allow you to give more voting power to specific roles in your server. For example, you might want moderators' votes to count more than regular members.

How does it affect polls?

When a user votes in a poll, the system checks their highest weighted role and assigns that weight to their vote. For example, if someone has the "Moderator" role with a weight of 3, their vote counts as 3 votes.

Example scenarios:
  • Equal voting: Set all roles to weight 1 (default)
  • Staff-weighted: Regular members (1), Moderators (2), Admins (3)
  • Seniority-based: New members (1), Members (2), Veterans (3)
  • VIP system: Members (1), Subscribers (2), VIP (3), Premium (5)
Important: Users with multiple roles get the highest weight from all their roles, not the sum of all weights.
